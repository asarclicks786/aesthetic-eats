🇵🇰 Pakistani Culinary Heritage

COURSE 01: APPETIZER

Shahi Silk Shami Kebab

⏱️ TIME: 60 Mins | 👨‍🍳 INVENTOR: Nawab's Royal Chefs | 📍 ORIGIN: Awadh, Old India

📜 History & Chronicle

"Yeh Kebab Awadh ke aik boht hi bimar aur boht barri umer ke Nawab ke liye un ke shahi bawarchiyoon ne ijad kiye thay. Nawab ke dant nahi thay magar unhain gosht khane ka boht shauq tha, is liye gosht aur dalon ko itna peesa gaya ke woh munh me jaate hi makkhan ki tarah ghul jayein."

📋 Ingredients & Process

Ingredients: Beef/Mutton 1kg, Chana Dal 250g, Whole Red Chillies, Garam Masala, Fresh Mint, Coriander.
Process: Gosht aur dal ko kharray masalon ke sath boil karein jab tak pani khushk na ho jaye. Isay chopper me nihayat bareeq pees lein. Chop ki hui sabz mirch, podina aur piyaz mila kar kebabs ki shape dein aur fry karein.

🇹🇭 Thailand Royal Culinary Arts

COURSE 02: MAIN COURSE

Tom Yum Goong (Thai Shrimp Soup)

⏱️ TIME: 30 Mins | 👨‍🍳 INVENTOR: Chao Phraya River Fishermen | 📍 ORIGIN: Central Thailand

📜 History & Chronicle

"Chao Phraya darya ke mwahay (fishermen) fresh jheengay pakarhte thay. Unho ne darya ke kinare ugne wali jade-bootiyan jese Lemongrass aur Galangal ko mila kar ye soup ubala taake thand aur beemariyon se bacha ja sakay."

📋 Ingredients & Process

Ingredients: Medium Shrimps 400g, Shrimp Stock 4 cups, Lemongrass, Galangal, Kaffir Lime Leaves, Thai Bird's Eye Chillies, Mushrooms, Fish Sauce, Lime Juice.
Process: Shrimp stock me lemongrass, galangal aur kaffir lime leaves daal kar ubalen. Mushrooms, chilies aur jheengay daal kar 3 mins pakhayein. Fish sauce aur lime juice dalen.
